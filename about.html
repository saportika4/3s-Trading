100% Waterproof Products

All WPC products from 3S Trading are completely waterproof — zero swelling, zero warping, even in high-humidity and outdoor environments.

Termite & Borer Proof

Our WPC material is completely resistant to termites, borers and wood rot — eliminating the biggest drawback of natural wood construction.
