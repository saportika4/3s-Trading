Everything you need to know about WPC

01 What is WPC and why should I choose it?

WPC (Wood Plastic Composite) is an eco-friendly material made from wood fibers and polymers. It combines the natural look of wood with the durability of plastic — 100% waterproof, termite-proof, and maintenance-free. It is the ideal alternative to conventional wood for doors, boards, decking and wall panels.

02 Are WPC products 100% waterproof?

Yes, all 3S Trading WPC products are 100% waterproof. They do not swell, warp, crack or rot when exposed to moisture or humidity. This makes them ideal for bathrooms, kitchens, outdoor spaces, and high-humidity environments.

03 What are the advantages of WPC over wood?

WPC products are termite-proof, borer-proof, moisture-proof, fire retardant, and completely maintenance-free. Unlike wood, WPC never requires painting, polishing, or regular upkeep. It is also eco-friendly as it is made from recycled wood waste and agricultural residues.

04 What products does 3S Trading offer?

3S Trading offers 17+ WPC and PVC products including: WPC/PVC Foam Sheets, WPC 3 Layer Sheets, WPC Doors & Door Frames, WPC Exterior & Interior Wall Panels, WPC Wall Cladding, WPC Decking, DIY Decking, WPC Planks, WPC Rafters, WPC Beams, WPC UV Printed Doors, WPC 3D Embossed Decking, WPC CO-EX Decking & Wall Panels.
